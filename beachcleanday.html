Join us for the biggest #2minutebeachclean ever!

On June 11th we’ll be down on the beach – or up a mountain, in a river or in the words – doing our #2minutebeachclean or #2minutelitterpick to celebrate our first ‘national’ day. The great thing about it is the fact that you don’t need any paperwork or insurance and there’s no set time. All you do is go out and do it! Pick up some litter for a couple of minutes and then snap it, tag it with #2minutebeachclean or #2minutelitterpick and bin it or recycle it to help make our world a nicer place.

  You might not think it will make much of a difference – and it may not on its own – but when you add them all together… that’s when good things start to happen.
